Soffit Installation

Soffit is used to cover the underside of an eave. The installation of the soffit will decide the location of the inside and outside corner posts.It is also required to finish the soffit before installing the last course of siding on the wall.

Daystar soffit is designed to be simply placed longitudinally from wall to fascia. Soffit panels are comparable to the vertical siding.Manufacturers make both solid and vented panels, as well as combinations of the two.

Soffits are made up of a variety of materials with varied features and advantages.

  • Wooden Soffit
  • Steel Soffit
  • Fibre Cement Soffit
  • Vinyl Soffit
  • Aluminium Soffit
  • UPVC Soffit
